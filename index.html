Welcome

Last update @ April 15, 2023

Welcome to my home page! I am a postgraduate research student at School of Economics, the University of Nottingham Ningbo China.

My research interests lie in the fields of behavioural and experimental economics as well as in applied microeconomics. I apply game theory and experiments (online and lab) to address my research questions. My research agenda focuses on decision-making and network formation in groups, and the effect of social identity, social norms and social nudge on social community development.
